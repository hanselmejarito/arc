Website Development Proposal

Professional, mobile-ready website — helps patients find the nearest ARC branch, learn about services, and contact the clinic fast, especially during animal bite emergencies.

The Challenge

ARC operates 11 branches across Nueva Ecija and Tarlac (ARC, LIFEMED, and VAXGUARD), but patients currently rely on Facebook pages and word-of-mouth to find locations, hours, and hotlines. After an animal bite, every minute counts — a centralized website reduces confusion and builds trust.
